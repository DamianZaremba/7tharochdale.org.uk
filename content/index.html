7thA Rochdale is a long established Scout Group near the centre of Rochdale, in Lancashire. Rochdale is the birthplace of celebrities such as Gracie Fields & Lisa Stansfield.

We are a mixed group based at Spotland Bridge, the grey block building with the double green gates. All three sections are currently operating; Beaver Scouts, Cub Scouts & Scouts.

The Beavers & Cubs meet on a Wednesday and the Scouts on a Thursday.

The leadership team is made up of a diverse selection of people including member's parents and Young Leaders from our district explorer unit.

We run a varied programme for the young people in the community and welcome new members from all backgrounds.
