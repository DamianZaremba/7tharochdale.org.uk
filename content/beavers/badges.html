--- layout: section-beaver title: Beavers - Badges ---

Badges are split into 5 categories;

Core

These badges are obtained on joining the movement, moving betwean units or for length of time spend in scouts.

Activity

These badges are obtained by completing a set of requirements in a specific field.

Staged

These badges are like activity badges but have multiple levels so a younger member with more experience might possess a higher level than their older counterpart.

Partnership

These badges aim for you to work in partnership with another group or external organization in order to achieve the badges.

Awards

Awards are achieved by trying new things and learning skills, these connect in with external awards such as DofE.

For the latest Beaver badges along with the requirements for obtaining them please see this scout base page or speak to a leader.
